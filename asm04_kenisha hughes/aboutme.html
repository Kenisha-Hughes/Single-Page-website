About Me

Hi, I’m Kenisha Hughes, a Computer Information Systems major at NCU. My journey is deeply rooted in my love for God, my family, and the values that shape who I am. As a proud member of the Seventh-day Adventist Church, my faith inspires and guides me in everything I do. When I’m not studying, I enjoy reading and traveling, especially exploring the beauty of Jamaica. These moments allow me to unwind and find inspiration in the world around me. My family is my greatest source of love and support, and they, along with my faith, keep me grounded as I work towards my dreams. Life is a journey of growth, service, and purpose, and I’m grateful for the opportunities to make a difference and live out the values that matter most to me.


Sports

Manchester United has been my favorite football club for as long as I can remember. There’s something about their legacy, resilience, and passion for the game that resonates deeply with me. Watching their matches is always an emotional rollercoaster, from the nail-biting finishes to the moments of pure brilliance on the pitch. I admire the club’s rich history and the legendary players who have worn the jersey with pride. From the iconic "Class of '92" to today’s stars, Manchester United represents a tradition of excellence and determination. Supporting the Red Devils isn’t just about the wins—it’s about the journey, the loyalty, and the love for the game. No matter the highs or lows, I stand by United with unwavering support. For me, being a Manchester United fan is more than following a team; it’s being part of a family united by passion for football. .
